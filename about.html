Our Story

Finding great vendors in Tucson shouldn't feel like a second job.

Anyone who's planned an event in Tucson knows the drill. You ask around, search through Facebook groups, leave voicemails that never get returned, and end up with a handful of half-answers right before your deadline.

Tucson has genuinely talented local vendors — caterers, photographers, DJs, decorators — who do incredible work. The problem was never the talent. It was the connection.

We built TEC to fix that. One request from a planner becomes instant, targeted outreach to the vendors most likely to be a great fit. No endless searching. No phone tag. No guesswork.

We're in our early days and proud of it — it means every vendor on this platform has been onboarded personally, and every consumer request gets real attention.

🎯

The Problem We're Solving

Tucson's event vendor market is fragmented. Great vendors are hard to find, and event planners waste hours on dead-end searches. We fix that with a single, structured request that reaches the right people instantly.

Simple for everyone involved.
