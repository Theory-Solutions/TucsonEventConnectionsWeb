Before You Book

What Most People Miss

Smart questions to ask before signing any vendor contract.

⚡ Access & Power

Inflatables require a dedicated 20-amp circuit and need at least a 4-foot gate for delivery access. Always confirm both before booking.

Read the FAQ →

🌿 Setup Surfaces

Tent anchoring differs on grass vs. pavement — concrete stakes vs. water barrels. Ask your rental company what's needed for your venue surface.

Read the FAQ →

🗑️ Clean-up & Trash

Many caterers don't haul away waste by default. Clarify who handles post-event cleanup and trash removal before you sign.
