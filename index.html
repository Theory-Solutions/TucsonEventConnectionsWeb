🌵 Tucson Event Pro-Tips

Ask these 3 questions before you book any vendor!

1. Access & Power

Confirm if they need a dedicated 20-amp circuit or a 4ft wide gate for Jumping Houses. Don't let a narrow gate stop the party!

2. Setup Surfaces

Will they be on grass or pavement? Tucson winds require different weighting systems for safety on hard surfaces.

3. Clean-up & Trash

For Catering, clarify if they haul away trash or if the host is responsible. Avoid hidden clean-up fees!
