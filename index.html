Insider Knowledge

Tucson Event Pro Tips

Ask these 3 questions before booking any vendor.

01
Access & Power

Confirm if they need a dedicated 20-amp circuit or a 4ft wide gate for inflatables. Don't let a narrow gate cancel the party!

02
Setup Surfaces

Grass or pavement? Tucson winds require different weighting systems. Hard surfaces need special anchoring for safety.

03
Clean-up & Trash

For catering, clarify who hauls away trash. This is the #1 hidden fee surprise at Tucson events.
