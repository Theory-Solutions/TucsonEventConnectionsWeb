Safety First

Before You Rent Inflatables

🚪
Check Your Gate Width

Inflatables need at least a 4ft wide gate to get into your backyard. This is the #1 reason for day-of cancellations — measure before you book.

⚡
Power Requirements

Every inflatable needs a continuous 20-amp outlet within 100 feet. No extension cord daisy chains. Ask about generator rental ($75–$125) if your outlet is too far.

🌡️
Heat & Surface Safety

Vinyl gets scorching hot in direct Arizona sun. Grass is ideal — concrete requires tarps underneath. Water slides are the smart summer pick.

FAQ

Inflatable Rental Questions

Standard bounce houses run $150–$250 per day. Water slides range $250–$450. Combo units are $300–$450. Most rentals include delivery, setup, and takedown within Pima County.

Standard bounce houses need a flat 15x15 ft area with 3 ft clearance on all sides and no trees or power lines overhead. Larger slides may need 20x35 ft. Vendors will confirm fit during booking.

You need a 20-amp outlet within 100 feet of the setup spot. If that's not possible, most companies rent generators for $75–$125. Never use extension cord chains — it's a fire risk.

Vinyl surfaces get dangerously hot in direct sun above 100°F. Water slides are ideal for summer events. Dry inflatables work best in morning/evening or under shade. Most companies won't operate above 110°F.
