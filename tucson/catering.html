What to Know

Before You Book a Caterer

🧹
Clarify Cleanup

Full-service caterers usually handle cleanup. Food trucks often leave trash to the host. Always confirm before signing — hidden cleanup fees are the #1 surprise in Tucson events.

⚡
Check Power Needs

Food trucks need a dedicated 20-amp circuit or generator access. Confirm your venue's electrical setup matches what the caterer requires — especially for backyard events.

🌡️
Plan for the Heat

Tucson summers hit 100°F+. Outdoor catering needs shade coverage, cold holding equipment, and plenty of hydration stations. October–April is prime season for outdoor food service.
