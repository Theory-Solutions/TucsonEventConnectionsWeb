What to Know

Before Booking a Photographer

🎨
Review Their Style

Every photographer has a distinct editing style — light and airy, dark and moody, documentary, or editorial. Review full galleries, not just highlight reels, before booking.

🌅
Tucson Golden Hour

The Sonoran Desert produces some of the most stunning golden hour light anywhere. Schedule portraits 1 hour before sunset for magical results — your photographer will thank you.

💾
Ask About Deliverables

Clarify what you're getting: number of edited photos, turnaround time, print rights, and whether raw files are included. This varies widely between photographers.

FAQ

Photography Questions Answered

Basic 2–4 hour coverage runs $300–$800. Full wedding packages are $800–$1,500 for 6–8 hours. Photo booth rentals are $400–$800. Videography starts at $600. Pricing depends on hours, deliverables, and experience level.

Most packages include the booth, an attendant, unlimited prints, digital copies, props, and a custom backdrop. Premium options add 360-degree video, GIF stations, and instant social media sharing.

Typical turnaround is 2–4 weeks for events and 4–8 weeks for weddings. Many photographers share sneak peeks within 48 hours. Rush delivery may be available for an extra fee.

Some offer photo + video combo packages at a discount. Others partner with videographers for bundled pricing. Ask during the quote process — combos usually save 15–25% vs. booking separately.
