What to Know

Before Booking Music

🔊
Venue Sound Rules

Many Tucson venues have noise ordinances and decibel limits — especially outdoor locations near residential areas. Confirm sound rules with your venue before booking your DJ.

📝
Share Your Playlist

Send your must-play and do-not-play lists 2-4 weeks before the event. Good DJs build smooth transitions around your favorites — they need prep time.

🔌
Outdoor Power Needs

Outdoor DJ setups need reliable power — dedicated circuits or generator access. Desert wind and dust also matter — confirm your DJ has outdoor-rated gear.
