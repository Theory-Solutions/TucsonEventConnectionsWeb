Pro Tips

Before You Book Rentals

🌡️
Arizona Sun Protection

Tucson events need serious shade. Budget for 20% more tent coverage than you think — guests will crowd under shade and you don't want anyone in direct sun.

📏
Measure Your Space

Know your exact yard or venue dimensions before requesting quotes. Vendors need this to recommend the right tent size and table layout.

🏗️
Setup Surfaces Matter

Grass vs. concrete requires different anchoring systems. Tucson winds can be strong — vendors need to know your surface type for safe tent installation.

FAQ

Table & Tent Rental Questions

Folding chairs run $2–$4 each, tables $8–$15 each. Premium chiavari chairs are $8–$12 each. Package deals for 50+ guests usually bring per-item costs down. Delivery fees typically add $50–$150.

Plan for 10–12 sq ft per seated guest, or 6–8 sq ft for standing. A 20x30 tent fits about 50 seated guests. For Tucson's intense sun, always oversize your shade by 20%.

Most Tucson rental companies include delivery, setup, and takedown in their pricing. They typically need 2–4 hours before the event for tent installation and will pick up the day after.

Many rental companies offer linens, centerpiece stands, and accent lighting as add-ons. Ask for a complete package quote — bundling usually saves 15–20% vs. sourcing separately.
