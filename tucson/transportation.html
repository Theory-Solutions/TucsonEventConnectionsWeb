What to Know

Before Booking Transportation

🍸
Alcohol & Safety

If your event serves alcohol, shuttles are essential for liability. Arizona DUI laws are strict — providing guest transport shows responsibility and prevents incidents.

⏰
Build in Buffer Time

Tucson traffic on I-10 and Oracle can add 15-30 minutes during rush hour. Always add 30-minute buffers to your transportation timeline.

📋
Confirm Capacity & Stops

Know your exact headcount and number of pickup/dropoff points. Multiple hotel pickups add time and cost — consolidating into fewer stops saves money.
