Pro Tips

Before Booking Decor

🌡️
Heat & Balloons

Latex balloons pop faster in Tucson's extreme heat. For outdoor summer events, use foil balloons or schedule installations for evening. Indoor events have no restrictions.

📐
Share Your Venue Details

Send photos and measurements of your space. Ceiling height, wall width, and entrance dimensions affect what installations are possible. Decorators design to your specific venue.

🎨
Color Coordination

Have your color palette ready. Exact balloon colors can be matched to Pantone codes. Share inspiration photos so the decorator nails your vision on the first try.

FAQ

Decor Questions Answered

Basic balloon bouquets start at $80–$150. Arches and installations run $200–$400+. Custom piñatas are $60–$200. Full event styling with backdrops and centerpieces ranges $400–$1,000+.

Custom balloon installations need 4–8 weeks. Full event styling needs 2–3 months. Simple bouquets and arrangements can often be done with 1–2 weeks notice.

Most include setup in pricing. Teardown varies — some include it, others charge extra. Balloon installations typically need 2–3 hours before the event. Always confirm the timeline with your venue.

Latex balloons degrade faster in extreme heat. For outdoor summer events, use foil/mylar balloons or plan installations after sunset. Indoor events have no heat concerns. Your decorator will advise on the best materials for your setup.
